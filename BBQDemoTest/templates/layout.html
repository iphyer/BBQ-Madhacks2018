About

BBQ standing bounding box qualifier which is a web service that examines the matching between ground truth bounding boxes and the predicted bounding boxes and produces a prediction quality report and debugging tools for object detection algorithm.

You can upload the image you want to debug and two sets of bounding box txt files to let BBQ tells you about the matching quality these two sets of bounding box txt files.

If you check the detected mismatched bounding boxes, BBQ will lead you to a more detailed page to check each mismatch clearly.

Check Github!
